Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when signing up as a volunteer, donor, or organization, such as:

  • Name, email, and phone number
  • Skills, donation types, or organization details
  • Usage data (e.g., page visits)

How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Connect you with relief efforts
  • Provide updates on your contributions
  • Improve our services

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Protection

We take steps to safeguard your data, including:

  • Secure storage practices
  • Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your data
  • Ask for corrections or deletion
  • Contact us at privacy@reliefrescue.org

This policy is effective as of April 1, 2025.
